The action of Harry Potter is set in a magical universe that exists in parallel with the world called Muggle. The world of magic is created by wizards or people who have been given the power to interact with others through magic. Most of them are members of the wizarding family. The community also includes squibs, i.e. people from wizarding families who do not display magical abilities, as well as wizards from Muggle families, called Muggle-borns. Things like spells, wands, potions, flying brooms, moving pictures and currencies are very popular items in the magic world. Rowling invented a series of her own concepts, objects, or means of communication, and the wizarding sport of Quidditch. Magic culture is hidden from Muggles, and magic is illegal in the presence of non-magicians. The action of the novel is concentrated in Great Britain. He has a local Ministry of Magic in London that manages and keeps the wizarding community a secret from Muggles. The main site of the action is the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, which educates young wizards - a castle in Scotland, which also houses a boarding school. Education at Hogwarts lasts seven years and starts at the age of eleven, and students are divided into four houses : Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw and Slytherin, named after the four founders of the school. Harry Potter's main action takes place between 1991 and 1998, with flashbacks and a 2017 epilogue for good measure. Each novel unfolds from the end of the summer holidays to the beginning of the next school year.
